Our Story

Every little moment

21st December 2025

The First Date 🍺

What began as a simple first date quietly turned into the start of something life-changing. Aniket was at his absolute best, and Tisha felt seen in a way that stayed with her long after the night ended. That evening didn't just create memories — it sparked a connection that would grow into a love story neither of them would ever want to end.

25th December 2025 — Christmas Day

The Hill, Bruno & a Kiss 🐶

Their second date blossomed into a cozy trek up a sunlit hill. Bruno, Aniket's energetic golden retriever, bounded ahead — happily stealing the spotlight. Between shared laughter, quiet glances under the wide sky, and the cool winter air, the day felt effortlessly special. On that hilltop with the city below, they shared a tender moment — a perfect memory they both knew would stay close to their hearts forever.

27th December 2025

Coffee, Dreams & a Bangle ☕

They sat across from each other in a quiet café, talking for hours about their lives, families, and dreams. On her wrist was the bangle he had given her — a deeply thoughtful gesture that made her heart soften in ways she hadn't expected. The way he believed in her and encouraged her brand launch gave her a new kind of strength. Their connection felt warmer, deeper, and undeniably more meaningful.

28th December 2025 ✦

She Said Yes 🌹

Aniket looked at Tisha with eyes that held all his hope and dreams, and asked her to be his girlfriend. Time seemed to pause, the world fading around them. With a teary smile and a voice full of warmth, she said yes — and in that instant, everything changed. They stood wrapped in a quiet, perfect certainty, silently promising to protect each other's hearts, to cherish every laugh, every tear, and every ordinary moment — together, forever.

New Year's Eve 2025

Their First New Year Together 🥂

Aniket surprised Tisha with a cozy staycation to start the year together. They got ready side by side, smiling at each other like nothing else in the world mattered — he looked sharp in his suit, and she glowed beside him. The night was a blur of drinks, laughter, and a midnight promise sealed with a kiss. But the quiet moments meant the most: staying up talking, sharing dreams, and waking up together on the first morning of the year. Two hearts, one love, choosing each other every single day.

Before the Goodbye

He Stayed Anyway 🫶

In the days just before Aniket was meant to leave for the USA, Tisha fell sick. Their time together slowed into something gentler and more intimate. Torn between plans and her, Aniket never stopped choosing to be by her side. When she met his friends, Tisha felt warmly included — like she truly belonged in his world. The way he kept checking on her, gently and constantly, made her feel deeply cared for and safe.

And then came the distance

8,700 Miles Apart, Hearts Closer Than Ever 📱

Different time zones, 8,700 miles apart — yet hearts closer than ever. Mornings began with sleepy video calls, and nights ended with whispered goodnights on the phone. They fought, they missed, they ached — but always found their way back to each other. "I miss you" and "I love you" became the bridges across the miles. Because what they shared was stronger than distance, deeper than fear. And with faith, love, and God's grace, they are stepping into their next chapter — together.
